The question no one can answer

Everyone knows they should be using AI. Almost no one knows where to start — with evidence.

The gap is not access to AI models. The gap is knowing which problem to solve first — with proof, from the people who live it, without your data ever leaving the building. The usual answers all fail:

  • Top-down strategy decks pick use cases in a boardroom, far from where the work actually hurts. They look good and rarely ship.
  • Consultants run interviews, hand over a slide pack, and leave. The knowledge walks out with them; nothing is owned, nothing runs.
  • Scattered enthusiasm — a few teams playing with chatbots — produces disconnected experiments that rot and leak data.

Hypsis Surface produces the missing answer: a ranked, evidence-backed map of where AI will pay off in your company, sourced from your own people and grounded in your own operations.

How it works

Four moves. One loop that gets smarter every time.

I

Anyone describes a pain point

Free text, a document, a screenshot. No form, no jargon. “Closing the month takes me three days of copy-paste.”

II

The AI refines it

It asks only the follow-ups you can answer — not which ERP issues the invoices or where the data lives.

III

The knowledge graph answers the rest

Questions about systems and process are answered from a sovereign company knowledge graph. What it doesn’t know yet, it learns — once, forever.

IV

The opportunities are ranked

Each pain point is scored on breadth, intensity, and buildability. The result is a map: act here first.

The more your organization uses it, the richer the knowledge graph — and the sharper the next ranking.
