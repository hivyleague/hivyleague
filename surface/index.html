The method

One method, three stages. Surface is where it starts.

Stage 01

Surface

Your teams expose the work that wastes their week. AI refines each pain point and ranks them by where a fix pays off first.

Stage 02

Build

Forward-deployed engineers — and your own people — turn the top pain points into working tools, on your box, in weeks.

Stage 03

Factorize

The best tools become shared, multi-user applications on one knowledge graph. Capability compounds across the company.

Every stage feeds the next — and the knowledge graph gets richer each time.
